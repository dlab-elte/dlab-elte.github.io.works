Our Projects

Assessing the benefits of study preregistration

Our goal is to assess the impact of introduction of preregistrations by comparing preregistered and non-preregistered studies along different descriptive features.
Details

Decision Attitude Questionnaire

The aim of this project is to develop a measurement tool to assess people’s propensity to commit mistakes in everyday life.
Details

Economic Inequality, Poverty, and Decision-making

In this project, we investigate how economic inequality and poverty influences decision-making processes and how these biased processes form the society.
Details

Exploring the Mechanisms of Cognitive Control

Our primary focus is to test the assumption that the experience of negative affect is the trigger of the increase in cognitive control.
Details

Transparent Psi Project

A fully transparent pre-registered replication study of precognitive detection of reinforcement using an expert consensus design
